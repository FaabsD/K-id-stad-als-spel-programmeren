Zaanse Schans Guesser

A quick introduction

In this multiple choice quiz you will see an image, on this image is a building that can be found on the Zaanse Schans, based on what you see in the image you have to guess which building it is. No worries, there is no time limit, so you can think about it. At the end you can see what your score is and save it under your name. Have fun guessing!
